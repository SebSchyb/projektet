School Trips

What can we offer in our school trips?

At Bastard you can swing by with your class for a visit in the weekdays until 4pm, where we have diffrent courses, depending on what you are looking for.

"Trivselsdag"

Focus on the well-being of the class, through fun and games that enhance communication and teamwork. Price: 40 kr. per student

"Kompetencedag"

Introduction to boardgames that focus on negociacion, conversations and enhancement of social competences. Price: 40 kr. per student.

"Vidensdag"

Boardgames with focus on knowledge such as historical dates, characters, geography or other professional topics. Price: 40 kr. per student.

Hire a Guru

It is possible when making a reservation to also hire one of our Board Game Gurus.

Our dedicated Game Gurus can introduce you to many of the games in our selection, and guide you through the rules. A Game Guru costs 550 DKK per hour. If you are a member there is a 40% discount lowering the price to 330 DKK per hour. Only the booker needs to be a member to get the discount

If the dedicated Guru(s) should know specific genre of games or be able to speak Danish, please say so in the comments. We cant guarantee that the Guru know the rules to a specific game, but then the guru will try to find something similar. And even though the Guru might know the rules for a specific game, we might not have it or it might not be available if other guests are playing it. Games can unfortunately not be reserved

The Guru is booked from the time the reservation starts unless other is asked for in the comments.

Bastard to Go

Do we ever take gaming beyond the walls of the café? Yes we do. We have been at Roskilde Festival in Dream City (a chilled part of the camping area) It has been hugely popular and helped us on our quest to infect the world with boardgaming, we have also attended Copenhell (Denmarks largest metal music festival) and other conventions. Apart from that were sometimes doing board game events around Copenhagen (and beyond) for companies, libraries, organisations and everything in between.

Does this mean that I can get you to come and introduce games at my place? Yes!

If youre an employee organising a game day at your company, an eccentric millionaire wanting to do a unique game event on top of Mount Everest or just a guy/girl with a great idea, then request Games n Gurus To Go by filling out the form found here and lets hear about it. At the moment were super busy building up the café at Huset-KBH, so we cant promise that we have the time. But you never know we will do what we can to help you out.
