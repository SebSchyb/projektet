A little bit about us

Bastard Café is located in the heart of Copenhagen and has been providing food, drinks, 'hygge' and board games since we opened our doors in 2014.

We are a space that prides itself on being open and inclusive and you will find students, families, geeks and friends meeting up here.

Location & opening hours

We have over 100 games that are are free to play all day, every day, featuring family classics, the newest releases and a selection that the best modern boardgames have to offer.

If that's still not enough then we have another 5000+ boardgames available for you to try for a small fee or as part of your membership.
