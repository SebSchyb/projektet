Reservations

About reservations

If you would like to guarantee a table then you can book a table here down at the bottom of the page.

The booking lasts for 3 hours, please notice that if you show up late without informing the staff that you are late, the table is most likely taken by someone else.

Bookings come with free gaming and one free drink for each guest.
A free drink is either a cup of tea or coffee, a bottle beer (Carlsberg or Tuborg Classic), a bottle of soda or lemonade, a muffin, or a cookie.

Prices

75 DKK per adult

30 DKK per child (from 12 years or younger)

Membership:
You get 40% discount if you have an active membership on the entire booking fee.

The membership fee is 150 DKK and covers one year. For a renewel of your membership before it expires, it only costs 125kr.
